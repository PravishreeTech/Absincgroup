Vice President, Human Resources

Dr. Niranjan M. Kumar, PDF, P.hD.

Masters in Management (Wharton Business School, University of Pennsylvania)

President & Founding Director

Dr. Niranjan Kumar is the President and Founding Director of ABSincgroup, a premier regulatory and technical consulting firm specializing in biologics, pharmaceuticals, cell and gene therapy, and medical devices. With a distinguished career spanning over 30 + years, Dr. Kumar has held senior leadership and global management roles across top-tier life sciences and global Biopharma companies, driving R&D, innovation, Quality & Regulatory compliance and operational excellence on a worldwide scale.

A visionary leader with deep scientific and regulatory insight, Dr. Kumar founded ABSincgroup to address the growing complexity of product development, FDA compliance, and manufacturing challenges in highly regulated sectors. Under his leadership, the company has built an elite network of scientific and regulatory experts, serving as a trusted advisor to numerous Fortune 500 clients, emerging biotechs, and multinational pharma companies. He has Six Sigma Black Belt certification and USP expert committee member.

Dr. Kumar has successfully led strategic partnerships, regulatory remediation programs, and product development initiatives that have transformed clients’ business outcomes — from resolving FDA Warning Letters and Consent Decrees to accelerating commercialization and GMP readiness. His track record includes successful consulting engagements across North America, Europe, and the Middle East.

Known for combining scientific precision with executive leadership, Dr. Kumar continues to expand ABSincgroup’s global impact through expert-led consulting in FDA readiness, cGMP manufacturing, quality systems, regulatory strategy, and technology transfer. Dr. Kumar published in peer reviewed journal and presented in national and international conferences.
