Services

Global Biopharma Consulting | Regulatory, Compliance, & Digital Solutions

ABSINCGROUP USA is a strategic consulting consortium serving the biopharmaceutical, pharmaceutical, biotechnology, medical device, and diagnostics sectors. With a global network of highly experienced consultants, we provide full-spectrum support across drug development, regulatory compliance, quality systems, and AI-driven analytics.

We specialize in high-stakes engagements—including FDA 483/warning letter remediation, regulatory submissions, cGMP readiness, and digital transformation of quality systems—across both emerging biotechs and Fortune 500 companies.

Regulatory Compliance & Remediation

  1. FDA 483s, Warning Letters & Consent Decree Support
  2. We offer rapid-response remediation strategies for FDA enforcement actions, backed by proven expertise in root cause analysis, CAPA, and regulatory liaison.
  3. Change Control & Investigations (Deviations, OOS, Complaints)
  4. Our team supports robust investigations, change control systems, and complaint handling—aligned with global regulatory expectations.
  5. Clinical & Post-Market Compliance
  6. Ensure your trial integrity and post-approval surveillance are audit-ready with our GCP, pharmacovigilance, and risk management solutions.

Manufacturing & Engineering

  1. cGMP Manufacturing / Manufacturing Engineering
  2. From facility design to ongoing cGMP operations, we help biologics, vaccines, and gene therapy firms establish compliant and scalable production systems
  3. Lean Six Sigma & Continuous Improvement
  4. Integrate performance improvement, cost reduction, and data-driven quality enhancement using certified Six Sigma Black Belt expertise.
  5. Product Development & Specification Setting
  6. Early-stage development support, from QTPP definition to regulatory-ready specification establishment

Quality Systems & Laboratory Services

  1. Quality Control (QC) & Assurance (QA)
  2. We offer comprehensive support for setting up or remediating QC and QA systems in line with ICH, FDA, and EMA guidelines
  3. OOS Investigations & Laboratory Data Integrity
  4. Detailed support for laboratory investigations, root cause analysis, and ensuring compliance with ALCOA+ principles
  5. Validation Engineering
  6. Specialists in process validation, equipment qualification (IQ/OQ/PQ), cleaning validation, and software/system validation

Third-Party & Regulatory Oversight

  1. Regulatory Inspection Readiness & 3rd-Party Audits
  2. Act as your independent oversight body for CROs, CMOs, and testing labs—ensuring alignment with agency requirements
  3. Certified Project Management Consultants (PMP)
  4. From site construction to launch execution, our certified PM professionals ensure milestone achievement, timeline adherence, and stakeholder alignment
